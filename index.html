About Us

From IP Expertise to Data Science Pioneers: Revitalizing Intellectual Property with Technology

We started our careers in the Intellectual property domain with tasks like prior art searches, office actions, patent drafting etc., amassing profound expertise in this specialized field. Transitioning to data science, we've engineered world-class machine learning and AI solutions for diverse clients, highlighting our innovation and adaptability. Despite our evolution, our commitment to our IP roots remains unwavering. We are impassioned to contribute back to the IP domain, which has experienced minimal technological disruption in recent years. Our mission is to spearhead the revitalization of this field with cutting-edge technology.
